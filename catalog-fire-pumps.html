Product Overview

Industrial-Grade Fire Pump Packages

DOOIL supplies a full range of fire pump packages — from single-pump assemblies to complex multi-pump skid systems — designed to meet the most stringent international standards including NFPA 20, UL 448, FM Approval, and KFI certification.

Our packages include electric motor-driven pumps, diesel engine-driven pumps, and jockey (pressure maintenance) pumps, all pre-engineered and factory-tested for rapid site integration.

  • ✔ NFPA 20 / UL / FM / KFI Certified
  • ✔ Horizontal Split-Case & Vertical Turbine Pump Types
  • ✔ Electric, Diesel & Combined Driver Options
  • ✔ Custom Skid Fabrication & Pre-wiring
  • ✔ Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) Included
  • ✔ Capacities up to 10,000+ GPM
