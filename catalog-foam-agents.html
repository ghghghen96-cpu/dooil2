Product Overview

Foam Suppression Storage Solutions

DOOIL provides a comprehensive range of foam concentrate storage and proportioning solutions — from atmospheric foam tanks and bladder tanks to proportioning skids — meeting NFPA 11, EN 13565, and international standards.

We supply AFFF, FFFP, AR-AFFF, Protein, FP, and Fluorine-Free foam concentrates, matched to the correct storage vessel configuration for your specific hazard class.

  • ✔ Bladder Tank & Atmospheric Tank Types
  • ✔ AFFF, AR-AFFF, FFFP, Fluorine-Free Options
  • ✔ NFPA 11 / EN 13565 Compliant
  • ✔ Inline, Balance Pressure & Premix Proportioning
  • ✔ Capacities from 100L to 100,000L+
  • ✔ Stainless Steel & Carbon Steel Construction
