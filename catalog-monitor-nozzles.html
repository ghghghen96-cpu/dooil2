Product Overview

High-Capacity Fire Monitors & Foam Systems

DOOIL supplies an extensive range of fire monitor nozzles — from manually operated portable monitors to fully automated, oscillating, self-seeking turret monitors — designed for protection of open hazardous areas, tank farms, and process units.

Our foam monitors and proportioning systems are engineered to deliver the correct foam-water mixture instantly and reliably under worst-case conditions.

  • ✔ Manual, Remote-Controlled & Automated Oscillating Types
  • ✔ Flow Rates: 500 LPM ~ 20,000+ LPM
  • ✔ Foam-Water & Water-Only Monitor Options
  • ✔ IP65/IP67 Rated Actuator Systems
  • ✔ NFPA 11 / EN 15493 Compliant
  • ✔ Corrosion-Resistant Stainless & Bronze Construction
