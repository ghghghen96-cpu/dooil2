Product Overview

Integrated Fire & Gas Detection Systems

DOOIL provides fully integrated Fire & Gas (F&G) detection system packages — from standalone addressable fire alarm control panels to plant-wide F&G Safety Systems with SIL 2/3 rated controllers, including point and open-path gas detectors, flame detectors, and smoke & heat detectors.

Our systems are engineered to IEC 61511 and NFPA 72 standards and are compatible with leading SCADA/DCS platforms via Modbus, HART, and Foundation Fieldbus.

  • ✔ SIL 2 / SIL 3 Rated F&G Controllers
  • ✔ Addressable & Conventional Panel Types
  • ✔ Fixed Point, Open-Path & Ultrasonic Gas Detectors
  • ✔ UV/IR & Multi-Spectrum Flame Detectors
  • ✔ ATEX / IECEx Zone 1 & Zone 2 Certified Sensors
  • ✔ IEC 61511 / NFPA 72 Compliant Systems
