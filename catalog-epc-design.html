Service Overview

Full-Scope EPC Engineering & Procurement

DOOIL offers comprehensive EPC (Engineering, Procurement, & Construction) support services for fire protection systems in industrial plant projects worldwide. Our engineering team works alongside Korean and international EPC contractors, providing technical expertise from FEED through handover.

We manage the full project lifecycle — from fire hazard analysis and system design to equipment procurement, vendor management, FAT supervision, site support, and commissioning assistance.

  • ✔ Fire Hazard Analysis (FHA) & Risk Assessment
  • ✔ Conceptual / FEED / Detail Engineering
  • ✔ Equipment Procurement & Vendor Management
  • ✔ Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) Supervision
  • ✔ Site Construction & Commissioning Support
  • ✔ As-Built Documentation & O&M Manuals
