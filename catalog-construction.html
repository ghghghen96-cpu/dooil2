Service Overview

Expert Installation & Construction

DOOIL provides end-to-end construction services for advanced fire protection systems. Our experienced site teams handle everything from mechanical piping and pump room assembly to electrical wiring and intelligent detection system integration.

We ensure that every installation meets the highest international standards (NFPA, UL/FM) and local safety regulations, providing a seamless transition from engineering design to a fully operational system.

  • ✔ Mechanical Piping & Sprinkler Installation
  • ✔ Fire Pump Room Assembly & Alignment
  • ✔ Electrical & Alarm System Wiring
  • ✔ Gas Suppression System Installation (Clean Agent)
  • ✔ Site Testing & Commissioning (TAB)
  • ✔ Safety Inspection & Certification Support
