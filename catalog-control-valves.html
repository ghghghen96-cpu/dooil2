Product Overview

Fire Control & Deluge Valve Systems

DOOIL supplies the full range of fire protection valves — deluge valves, pre-action valves, check valves, OS&Y gate valves, butterfly valves, and test & drain assemblies — sourced from world-leading manufacturers and compliant with NFPA 13, 15, and 16.

Our valve packages include solenoid trim, hydraulic actuation trim, and manual release stations, fully pre-assembled and factory-tested for site-ready installation.

  • ✔ Deluge, Pre-Action & Alarm Check Valves
  • ✔ UL Listed / FM Approved / LPCB Certified
  • ✔ Electric, Hydraulic & Pneumatic Actuation
  • ✔ Pre-assembled Trim Packages
  • ✔ Full Range: DN50 ~ DN300 (2" ~ 12")
  • ✔ High-Pressure Rated up to 25 bar
